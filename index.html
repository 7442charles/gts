About Us

HISTORY AND FOUNDATION OF GTS

GTS is a charity non-profit organization registered with the government of Kenya under the Societies Act as Society. It works cooperatively with the community and other volunteers to reach out to the less privileged, the aged and street children in a mission to offer guidance and counseling, support the young less privileged to unleash their potential curbing the bondage of drug addiction, and give hope to the vulnerable as a way of giving back to the community.

Founded in 2017 by a group of Christian students in Kirinyaga, Kenya that work passionately to ensure that the young generation is out from addiction bondage (drug abuse). We anticipate and solve the complex barriers keeping young people from education and provide relevant development opportunities to the communities.

To make this possible we forge a powerful partnership with young people, governments, businesses, NGOs, philanthropy, civil society organization to develop and deploy solutions to unleash the potential of the next generation. What’s brings us together is our faith and the love of God and therefore every action we take underlies the manifestation of God’s love.

MISSION: Reaching out to the less privileged and giving back to the community.

    CORE VALUES:
  • Integrity

  • Commitment

  • Stewardship

VISION:To be a wellspring of hope and grace carrier to this generation and the generation to come.
